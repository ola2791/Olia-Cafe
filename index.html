Coffee

Cappuccino

Coffee drink composed of a single espresso shot and hot milk, with the surface topped with foamed milk

Iced Coffee

A creamy and popular cold beverage that is made from 3 main ingredients uger, coffee, and coled milk shaken throwen in a few ice cubes

Hot Chocolate

A hot drink made with dark, semisweet, or bittersweet chocolate grated or chopped into small pieces and stirred into milk with addition of suger

Latte

A milk coffee that is a made up of one or two shots of espresso, steamed milk and a final, thin layer of frothed milk on top.

Americano

Combines freshly pulled shots of espresso with hot water to achieve the size of a standard cup of brewed coffee.

Espresso

A single shot of coffee, usually served in a small demitasse cup

Mocha

A shot of espresso is combined with a chocolate powder or syrup, followed by milk or cream.

Frappe

An iced beverage that has been shaken, blended or beaten to produce a tasty, foamy, and refreshing drink. It is served cold, often with whipped cream and toppings

Turkish Coffee

Finely ground coffee beans with water (and often sugar) and bringing the liquid to a frothy foaming stage
